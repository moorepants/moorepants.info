We developed both direct and indirect identification methods and methods for identifying a gain gait scheduled controller for planar walking. The motivation was to develop a controller that could possibly be used in powered lower extremity prostheses, such as an exoskeleton. {% endmark %} Publications ============ - Moore JK, Hnat SK, van den Bogert AJ.
