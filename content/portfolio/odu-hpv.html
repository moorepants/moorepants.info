This project came out of my new found interest in bicycling everywhere instead of sitting in Hampton Roads auto traffic and appropriate technology. There also happened to be a guy in my class that I always saw riding around in a long wheelbased recumbent. I approached him about the project and the rest was history. We ended up doing pretty good in competition. I documented most everything on the `team website `_. {% endfilter %}
