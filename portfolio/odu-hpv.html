ODU Human Powered Vehicle

For my senior project at ODU my teammates and I designed a human powered vehicle to race in the annual ASME HPV competition. This project came out of my new found interest in bicycling everywhere instead of sitting in Hampton Roads auto traffic and appropriate technology. There also happened to be a guy in my class that I always saw riding around in a long wheelbased recumbent. I approached him about the project and the rest was history. We ended up doing pretty good in competition. I documented most everything on the team website.
